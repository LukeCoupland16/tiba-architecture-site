## 3. Site Map & Page Hierarchy

The site follows a minimalist, high-impact structure:

1. **Home:** The cinematic entry point.
2. **Works:** Traditional project categorization (Residential, Hospitality, Cultural, Commercial).
3. **Philosophy:** Dedicated space for the "Designing Freely" narrative.
4. **The Process:** A narrative breakdown of the architectural journey.
5. **Materials & Craftsmanship:** Interactive showcase of timber, curves, and textures.
6. **Contact:** Minimalist "Join the Journey" portal.

---

## 4. Key Functional Features

### 4.1 The "OH" Inspired Hero

* **Functionality:** Utilize the **dual-state hover effect** seen on `oharchitecture.com.au`.
* **Visual:** Large-scale facade renders that transition into interior/atmospheric renders upon hover.
* **Motion:** Slow, cinematic transitions. The transition should feel like an "unfolding" rather than a sudden change.

### 4.2 Interactive Material Palette

* **Concept:** A dedicated section or landing block focused on the "warmth of timber" and "fluidity of curves."
* **Interaction:** A grid of materials (Timber, Stone, Earth, Light) that expands or reveals high-resolution detail on hover, accompanied by a single word or short phrase from the brand story (e.g., "The Earth grounds you")^^.

### 4.3 The Five Elements (Symbolic Implementation)

* **Integration:** The elements (Fire, Earth, Air, Water, Ether) will not be used for project filtering but rather as **narrative anchors**^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Example:** A scroll-based interaction where a project image fades into the background as an "Element" symbol appears with a brief quote, such as "Wind moves through the trees and your thoughts"^^.
