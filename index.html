About Tiba

William Dair

Founder & Principal Architect

William Dair founded TIBA with a single conviction: that architecture should be an act of freedom. Trained across Europe and immersed in the living landscape of Bali, he brings a philosophy rooted in nature, material honesty, and the quiet power of space. Every project begins not with a brief, but with a question — what does this place want to become?

"When we slow down, we begin to truly see — the light, the textures, the atmosphere around us. Nature is my greatest source of inspiration."
